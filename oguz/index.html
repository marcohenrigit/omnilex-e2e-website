Automated Intake

New requests are automatically registered and assigned to the relevant client and supervising lawyer.

Matter Assessment

Based on the nature of the case, a tailored plan for handling the matter is developed. The required tasks are identified, structured, and prepared for legal examination.

Legal Examination

Omnilex researches the applicable law, relevant case law, and internal best practices, while assessing the client’s specific circumstances. The most effective way forward is identified and critically evaluated from all relevant perspectives.

Deliverable Creation

Depending on the case, all necessary documents and deliverables are drafted and prepared for the supervising lawyer’s approval.

Lawyer Sign-Off

The supervising lawyer retains full oversight and control throughout the process. Before any deliverables are sent to the client, the lawyer reviews and approves them.

Client Communication

Throughout the process, Omnilex communicates with the client whenever necessary to obtain additional information, clarify instructions, and gather input on the preferred strategy.
