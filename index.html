Overview

Not another tool. The work itself, done.

The AI Native Legal End-to-End Service is a managed legal service. Rather than only equipping your team to work faster, Omnilex takes defined legal matters off your desk and returns finished, review-ready output.

Every matter runs on the same AI-native engine your team already trusts for research — now applied across the full lifecycle of a matter, from first intake to delivered document, and supervised throughout by qualified Swiss lawyers.

You keep oversight and final sign-off. We carry the load in between.

How it works

From matter to finished output — in four steps.

01

Intake

You hand over a matter in plain language. Structured intake captures the facts, scope, and constraints up front — without the usual back-and-forth.

02

AI-native execution

Research, analysis, and first-draft work are produced on the Omnilex engine — grounded in Swiss law, fully sourced, in hours rather than weeks.

03

Lawyer review

Qualified Swiss lawyers review, refine, and stand behind every output. Nothing leaves without human accountability.

04

Delivery

You receive finished, review-ready work in your format, with a clear audit trail from source to result.
